Walt

Walt is a cranky male gray kangaroo who has a some sort of scar on his face, and bandages on his arms. His orginal catchphrase is pockets, and his birthday is April 24th.

Rooney

Rooney is the other cranky male kangaroo who is a lightish blue. He appears to have red boxing gloves on, and has some hair. His orginal catchphrase is punches, and his birthday is December 1st.

Astrid

Astrid is a snooty kangaroo who is mostly white with a green star mark on the outside of her eye. Her catchphrase is my pet and her birthday is September 8th.

Mathilda

Mathilda is also a snooty and is almost completly black. The inside of her ears are purple, and same with her mouth. Her catchphrase is wee baby and her birthday is November 12th.

Marcie

Marcie is a normal villager who is pink with some hair on her head. Her catchphrase is pouches, and her birthday is May 31.

Sylvia

Sylvia is a sisterly kangaroo who is purple and has blue ears. Her catchphrase, in New Horizons, is boing, and her birthday is May 3rd.

Kitt

Kitt is a normal villager, who is light brown, with different shades over her head. Her catchphrase is child, and her birthday is October 11th.

Carrie

Carrie, is a goldenish normal type who has blue eyeshadow. Her catchphrase is little one, and her birthday is December 5th.
